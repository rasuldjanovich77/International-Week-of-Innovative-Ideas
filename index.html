About INNOWEEK

About Us

The Week of innovative ideas INNOWEEK.UZ has been held annually since 2018. It is attended by local and foreign companies, investors, representatives of foreign diplomatic missions, entrepreneurs, as well as representatives of ministries and departments. Over the past years, many memorandums of cooperation and a number of successful investment contracts have been signed during the week. The week has become a reliable platform for demonstrating and promoting the most advanced innovative technologies, products, projects, prototypes or models for various business areas of domestic and foreign representatives. The purpose of the week is to exchange views among foreign and domestic experts and analysts on reforms in the field of higher education, science and innovation in New Uzbekistan, their essence, achievements and promising tasks in the field of innovative development.
